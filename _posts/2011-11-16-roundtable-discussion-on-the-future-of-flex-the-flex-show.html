This week, the episode of The Flex Show podcast is about the future of Flex following the announcement on Flash Player Mobile being killed. The podcast is in a roundtable format, with me, Sim Bateman, and Ashkay Dave. This was recorded prior to the blog post from Deepa and Andrew Shorten, and they are planning on organizing another one to discuss those announcements as well.

For now though, go get the podcast! It has lots of good opinions and ideas, and really gets into flex in the real world.

All for now!
